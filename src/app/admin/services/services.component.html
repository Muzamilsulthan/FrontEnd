Our Services

At RevTaskManagement, we offer a range of services to cater to different user roles and their specific requirements.

For Users

  • Secure Account Management: Create and manage your accounts with ease. Update your profile as needed.
  • Personal Task Management: Create, edit, view, and delete your tasks. Organize them into categories and set priorities to stay on top of your schedule.
  • Milestone Tracking: Define and manage milestones to track your task progress effectively.
  • Task Delegation: Assign tasks to others and keep track of their progress.
  • Organizational Tools: Utilize labels, categories, and sorting options to keep your tasks organized and easy to manage.
  • Notifications: Stay informed with timely notifications for upcoming tasks and deadlines.

For Admins

  • User Account Management: Create, update, and deactivate user accounts (Project Managers and Team Members). Assign appropriate access levels to ensure secure and efficient task management.
  • Reporting Tools: Access comprehensive reporting tools to monitor user activity and track task completion, helping you stay informed and make data-driven decisions.

For Project Managers

  • Client and Project Management: Manage client information and project details, including tasks completed and effort invested. Ensure that team members are allocated appropriately for project success.
  • Team Management: Add team members to projects and assign tasks based on historical data to predict the estimated effort required for each task.
  • Secure Login: Log in securely and reset passwords as needed.
